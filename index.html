Who We Are

NextSynergy Tech is a Charlotte, NC based technology company specializing in web & mobile development, AI solutions, cybersecurity, and tech education. Founded by Eshetu T. Wekjira, we bridge education and industry.

We operate NextSynergy Learning Academy, providing specialized training in AI, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and full-stack development — empowering the next generation of tech professionals.

From individual learners to enterprise clients across the US and Ethiopia, we deliver innovative digital solutions that create real impact.
